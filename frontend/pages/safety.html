Safety Policy

We are committed to ensuring the safety of our customers, delivery partners, and restaurant partners at every step of the process.

Your safety and trust are our top priorities.

1. Food Safety & Hygiene

  • All restaurants must follow strict hygiene standards
  • Food must be freshly prepared and properly packed
  • No expired or unsafe ingredients are allowed

2. Contactless Delivery Safety

  • Contactless delivery option is available
  • Orders can be left at your doorstep or selected location
  • Minimizes physical contact for safety

3. Rider Safety Guidelines

  • Riders must follow traffic rules and regulations
  • Helmet and safety gear are mandatory
  • No reckless driving or unsafe behavior allowed
  • Respectful behavior towards customers is required

4. User Safety Rules

  • Users must provide accurate delivery information
  • Fake orders or harassment is strictly prohibited
  • Abusive behavior may result in account suspension

5. Payment Security

  • Secure payment gateways are used
  • Never share OTP or card details with anyone
  • Fraud detection systems are active

6. Data Protection

  • User data is encrypted and securely stored
  • Only necessary data is shared with partners
  • No unauthorized access is permitted

7. Delivery Safety

  • Food is handled with care during transportation
  • Sealed packaging is recommended
  • Orders are protected from contamination

8. Emergency Support

  • Report any unsafe delivery or behavior immediately
  • 24/7 support is available for critical issues
  • Quick action is taken on safety complaints
Note: We continuously improve our safety standards to ensure a secure experience for all users.
